About Us

THE COMPANY

Yata Jasamedia with Techvend Rapid Solution Sdn Bhd is Company partner, company incorporated in Malaysia with the purpose of developing customised solutions in the automatic vending machines and payment system industry. We can provide vending solutions from designing, conceptualising and up to the manufacturing of the finished machine.

Over the years, we have acquired the expertise and capability to engage in the manufacture of vending machines for phonecards (physical cards or soft pins), drinks, confectionery, stationery, postal stamps and parking and ticketing equipment.

Within Yata Jasamedia and its Associated Companies, the Group is able to undertake projects & development of customized solutions specializing in RFID, GSM/ GPRS & wireless communications. Providing total solutions, system consult, system integration and installation of home and commercial security, CCTV, Alarm Monitoring, Access Control System, Time Attendance System, Electronic Identification, Integrated Security Management System and Car Park System.

OUR CUSTOMERS

With vast experience of more than 12 years in manufacturing and constant investment in machinery and R&D, we have the advantage in providing high quality products and better service to our satisfied customers. Over the years, we are able to develop strong working relationships with them and have successfully marketed our products throughout South East Asia, China, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Cambodia, South America, Middle East and Africa.
