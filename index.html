Datastream Technologies Sdn Bhd is a trusted Malaysian company specializing in developing high-tech vending machines and advanced payment solutions. We proudly partner with Yata Jasamedia to deliver cutting-edge technology from concept to creation.

About Us

With over 12 years of experience in vending innovation, our company has successfully designed, built, and installed machines for a variety of uses including phone cards, drinks, parking, ticketing, and more. Our team of experts combines hardware development, software customization, and system integration to meet the needs of customers across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and beyond.

Our Products

  • Phonecard Vending Machines (Soft Pin & PVC)
  • Custom Beverage & Snack Vending Units
  • Parking & Ticketing Machines
  • Refurbished & Used Vending Machines
  • Ice Bank Cooler, Dispenser, Ice-Tea Trolley Bar

Our Services

  • Full Machine Conceptualization & Manufacturing
  • System Consultation & Software Customization
  • Security Integration: CCTV, Access Control, RFID
  • Repairs, Maintenance & Spare Part Supply
  • Time Attendance & Electronic Identification Systems
